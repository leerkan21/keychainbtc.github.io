Security Basics

Understanding Bitcoin security is essential to protect your funds. Here are the basics every user should know:

  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
  • Always back up your wallet and keep your seed phrase safe.
  • Beware of phishing websites and scams—never share your private key.
  • Keep your software and firmware up to date.
