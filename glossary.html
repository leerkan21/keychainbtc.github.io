Bitcoin Glossary

Quick reference for key terms and concepts used in the Bitcoin world:

  • Address: A string of letters and numbers used to send or receive Bitcoin.
  • Private Key: A secret code that gives access to your Bitcoin.
  • Public Key: A code linked to your private key, used to receive Bitcoin.
  • Node: A computer that supports the Bitcoin network.
  • Block: A record of Bitcoin transactions added to the blockchain.
  • Blockchain: A public, immutable ledger of all Bitcoin transactions.
