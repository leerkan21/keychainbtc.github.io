Bitcoin Glossary

Address
A string of letters and numbers that represents a destination on the Bitcoin network. Used to send or receive Bitcoin.
Blockchain
A decentralized, public ledger that records all Bitcoin transactions in chronological order.
Node
A computer connected to the Bitcoin network that helps validate transactions and blocks.
Private Key
A secret number that allows you to spend Bitcoin from your wallet. Keep it safe and never share it.
Seed Phrase
A list of 12 or 24 words that can be used to recover your wallet. This is your backup—store it offline securely.
